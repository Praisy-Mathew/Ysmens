Our Core Principles

Objectives of the Association

Support the YMCA

To function primarily as service clubs for the YMCA, supporting its mission and programs in communities worldwide.

Civic & Social Justice

To support other worthy organisations and encourage justice in civic and international affairs, abstaining from party politics.

Active Fellowship

To cultivate good fellowship and keep members informed on and actively involved in religious, civic, economic, and social matters.

Active Service to Build a Better World

Welcome to Y's Men International

Y’s Men International is oriented to serving the worldwide YMCA and the communities in which it is present throughout the world. Founded in Toledo, Ohio, USA in 1922 to support a local YMCA, Y’s Men International is now oriented to serving the worldwide YMCA, the world’s largest non-governmental youth organisation.

Our Motto & Core Values

"To acknowledge the duty that accompanies every right." Our motto is a constant challenge to us to pay less heed to our rights, and more to the discovery and discharge of their corresponding duties.

YMCA Partnership

We strive through active service to develop, encourage and provide leadership to build a better world for all humankind, in partnership with and supporting the YMCA.
