Guidelines

Helping Tips to Organise Regional Projects

Follow these key points to ensure the successful execution and reporting of all regional projects across the district.

  • Display the Project logo prominently in the program.
  • Invite the concerned DSD or RSD of that portfolio to the program (Even if there is another guest).
  • Take photographs of the event and ensure they are sent with your report.
  • Do regular reporting to maintain transparency and tracking.

Community Support Initiatives

“Home for the homeless”

Objective: Provide safe and dignified housing for the homeless and vulnerable populations.

  • 1. Building New Homes Construct new dwellings for those without shelter.
  • 2. Renovation of Old Homes Upgrade existing homes lacking basic facilities.
  • 3. Completing Incomplete Homes Provide support to finish partially built homes.

This project aims to bring hope and shelter to those in need. Let’s work together to make a difference!
