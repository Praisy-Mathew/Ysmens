Lieutenant Regional Director’s Message

Dear Y’s Men Family,

Greetings to all members of District 1.

It gives me immense pleasure to convey my best wishes through this District Bulletin as we commence another exciting year of service, fellowship, and leadership. I extend my heartfelt congratulations and warm wishes to our District Governor, Ym. PWAF Rahul S. Kumar, and his team of dedicated officers as they assume the responsibility of leading District 1 during this Y’sdom year.

The strength of our movement lies in the commitment and collective efforts of its members. With a capable leadership team at the helm, I am confident that District 1 will continue to scale new heights in membership growth, meaningful service projects, leadership development, and fellowship.

As the Lieutenant Regional Director of Zone I, I assure the District Governor, district officers, club leaders, and all members of my wholehearted support, guidance, and cooperation throughout the year. I remain committed to working closely with the district leadership and extending all possible assistance to ensure the successful implementation of programmes and the achievement of our common goals.

“To acknowledge the duty that accompanies every right.”

Ym. PWAF. Adv. Aby Thomas

District One

District Governor’s Message

Dear Y’men, Y’m Leaders, and Friends of District One,

Greetings of Peace, Friendship, and Service!

As I step into the role of your 6th District Governor for this landmark year, I am filled with immense gratitude, humility, and a profound sense of purpose. Our journey together in District One, nestled in the culturally rich and vibrant Central Travancore region, has always been defined by our collective passion. For me personally, this journey began when I joined this incredible family in 2011. Having served as a club President and walked alongside many of you over the last decade and a half, I have seen firsthand the miracles we can achieve when we work as one.

This year, our guiding stars will be three powerful pillars: Friendship, Unity, and Service.

To me, leadership is not about a position; it is about people. I have always strived to maintain deep, personal friendships with every single member—every “club cub”—of District One. This year will be no different. I want to assure you that my door is always open, and my hand is always extended in friendship. It is this bond of personal connection that will unite us, and it is our unity that will supercharge our service.

Our Vision for the Year: Signature Projects

To translate our unity into impactful action, we are launching two monumental projects this year that will define the legacy of District One:

The International Project: A Dedicated Dialysis Unit

In a major step toward healthcare accessibility, we will be establishing a state-of-the-art Dialysis Unit proudly bearing the name of District One. This life-saving international project will stand as a testament to our global vision and localized care, providing critical support to patients fighting kidney ailments.

The District Project: A Model Village

Closer to home, we are embarking on a holistic community-building initiative to develop a Model Village. This project will focus on sustainable development, basic infrastructure, health, and education, transforming an entire community and setting a benchmark for what true community service looks like.

Moving Forward Together

No single individual can change the world, but a united district can reshape communities. I call upon every club, every president, and every member to bring your energy, your ideas, and your heart to the table this year. Let us nurture our friendships, stand rock-solid in our unity, and elevate our service to heights District One has never seen before.

Thank you for your trust, your dedication, and your unwavering support.
Let us make this year truly unforgettable.

Yours in Friendship and Service,

Ym. PWAF Rahul S. Kumar

District Governor, District One Central Travancore Region
