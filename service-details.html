    What You’ll Learn

    Whether you're a beginner or an aspiring equestrian, our workshops cover the following key areas:

    • 1

      Grooming Basics

      Learn how to brush, clean hooves, detangle manes, and recognize signs of good coat health.

    • 2

      Feeding & Nutrition

      Understand what a balanced equine diet looks like and how to manage feed schedules

    • 3

      Saddling & Tack

      Discover how to correctly saddle a horse and maintain riding gear like bridles and reins.

    • 4

      Stable Management

      Get tips on keeping stables clean, safe, and comfortable for horses

    Bond, and Grow with Horses

    Caring for a horse is more than just feeding and riding—it’s about building trust, understanding their needs, and keeping them healthy and happy. Our Horse Care Workshops are designed to equip horse lovers of all ages with essential knowledge and hands-on experience.
